# Dave Messner for St. Louis County Assessor - Platform (Unofficial Reference)

Source: Dave Messner Campaign

## General Vision (Tone & Values)
**Vibe:** Fair, proactive, accurate, transparent, St. Louis grounded, professional.
**Core Message:** "Restoring fairness, predictability, and transparency to property assessments."
**Focus:** Everyday impact—property values, tax burdens, communication, modernizing systems.
**Approach:** Professional, accessible, honest. Local, inclusive phrasing ("Here in St. Louis County...", "From homeowners to business owners...").

## Fair Assessments
**Summary:** Ensure consistent valuation standards across neighborhoods and property classes.
**Key Points:**
- The real estate market changes faster than policy and systems can keep up.
- We need consistent valuation standards across all neighborhoods.
- Fairness for both residential and commercial property classes.
**Why It Matters:**
- Inconsistent assessments lead to unfair tax burdens.
- Every property owner deserves to be treated equally under the law.

## Proactive Service
**Summary:** Improve communication with property owners before reassessment cycles to reduce confusion and appeals.
**Key Points:**
- Communicate with property owners *before* reassessment cycles.
- Reduce confusion and the need for appeals.
- My door is open, my phone is on.
**Why It Matters:**
- Homeowners and business owners deserve expertise, competence, and communication.
- Proactive outreach builds trust in the Assessor's office.

## Accurate Data
**Summary:** Invest in updated systems and modern valuation tools to improve reliability and reduce errors.
**Key Points:**
- Invest in updated systems and modern valuation tools.
- Improve reliability and reduce errors in property valuation.
- Use national best practices and technology.
**Why It Matters:**
- Accurate data is the foundation of a fair assessment.
- Errors in valuation directly impact the taxes individuals and businesses pay.

## Transparent Process
**Summary:** Provide clearer explanations of valuation methodology and easier access to appeal information.
**Key Points:**
- Provide clearer explanations of how values are determined.
- Make access to appeal information easier.
- Be a fair and honest dealer in leading the Assessor's office.
**Why It Matters:**
- Transparency is essential for public trust.
- Residents should understand how their property values are calculated.

## Candidate Background & Experience
**Summary:** Dave brings a significant background in real estate, economic development, and community development to the position. He is not a career politician, but a Real Estate Professional.
**Key Qualifications:**
- **Real Estate & Appraisal:** Deep expertise in Real Estate Appraisal and Residential Real Estate.
- **Education & Character:** Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.) from Washington University in St. Louis. Master's in Dispute Resolution and Bachelor's in History from SMU. Proud Eagle Scout.
- **Economic Development:** Former Project Leader at Greater St. Louis, Inc., where he designed and executed strategic regional economic development programs and implemented organization-wide technology (Salesforce CRM).
- **Business Leadership:** Managing Partner at New Legacy Development Partners (adaptive re-use, complex financing, downtown development). Founder of Old Judge Coffee Company (re-founding an iconic 1850s St. Louis brand). Owner of Hermann Boutique Lodging.
- **Advocacy:** Committed to being a vocal advocate to local, county, and state government on policy decisions that impact the tax burden on St. Louis County residents.
